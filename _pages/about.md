Hi there!

I am **Esteban Carisimo**, a **Network Protocols Engineer on the Protocol Team at [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/)**, where I work on making the Internet faster. After over a decade in academic research studying how the Internet is built and how it performs, I now apply my measurement expertise and research frameworks to protocol engineering and performance.

Previously, I spent my academic career studying Internet infrastructure -- Submarine Cable Networks (SCNs), Internet Exchange Points (IXPs), Content Delivery Networks (CDNs), and the role of governments in network shaping and public-sector content delivery -- with the goal of contributing to a more resilient, open, and equitable Internet. I earned my Doctoral degree (2020) and Engineering degree (2014) from the [Universidad de Buenos Aires](https://www.uba.ar/#/), where I also served as an Assistant Professor in the [School of Engineering](https://www.fi.uba.ar) from 2020 to 2021. Growing up in Latin America has strongly shaped my interest in the region's Internet evolution.
